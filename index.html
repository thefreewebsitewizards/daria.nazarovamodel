About Me

Daria Nazarova is a New York–based plus-size model and creative force redefining modern beauty standards. Originally from Siberia, she has built an international career across editorials, campaigns, and runways, with features in Harper's Bazaar, L'Officiel, OK! Magazine, and more.

On the catwalk, she has graced New York Fashion Week, Swim Week, and Bridal Fashion Week, while off the runway she serves as a jury member and speaker at global fashion and beauty events. With every project, Daria delivers not just images but a message: confidence and style have no size.
